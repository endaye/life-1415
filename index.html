About Us

关于 壹肆壹伍

* * * *

Here at 1415 we strive to provide the best quality food. We offer traditional northeastern Chinese cuisine for delicious dining, takeout and delivery to Chicago. 1415 is named from our location (1415 W Fullerton Ave), which it is a lucky number for us.
